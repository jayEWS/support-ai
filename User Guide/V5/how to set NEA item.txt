## **Step-by-Step Tutorial: Adding a New Item (NEA)**



### **Step 1: Log in to the Backend**

1. Open your browser and navigate to the backend URL:

   ```

   for example https://t1.equipweb.biz/opstest/Setup/new/ >> you may open your backend accordingly 

   ```

2. Log in with your credentials.



### **Step 2: Navigate to Product Setup**

1. Once logged in, hover over **Product Setup** in the menu.

2. From the dropdown list, click on **Product Setup** to open the product management page.



---



### **Step 3: Click 'Add New'**

1. On the Product Setup page, locate and click the **Add New** button.

2. This will redirect you to the form for adding a new product.



---



### **Step 4: Fill in Basic Product Information**

On the Add New Product page, fill out the following fields:

1. **SKU ID**: Enter the unique SKU number for the item (e.g., `544905-01`).

2. **Item No**: Enter the same SKU ID here.

3. **Name**: Provide the product name/description (e.g., "Dyson Pure Cool TP11 (White/White)").

4. **Category**: Select the appropriate category from the dropdown menu (e.g., Fan).

5. **Barcode**: Leave this field blank unless specified.

6. **Product Description**: Add any relevant details about the product.

7. **Product Type**: Choose "Product - Track Inventory" unless otherwise instructed.

8. **UOM (Unit of Measure)**: Select the unit of measure (e.g., Each).

9. **Selling Price**: Enter the retail price of the product.

10. **Cost Price**: Leave this as `0` if not applicable.

11. **Minimum Selling Price**: Usually matches the selling price unless specified otherwise.



> **Note:** If the product is discontinued, check the box next to **Product is discontinued (cannot be purchased)**. >> its use for purchase ordering inventory web.



---



### **Step 5: Manage Attributes**

1. Scroll down to the **Attributes** section.

2. Click on **Show More** to view additional options.

3. Locate the **Redeem SG Applicable** field:

   - Select **Yes**, if the NEA has already been approved for this SKU.

   - Leave it blank if the SKU is still pending NEA approval.



---



### **Step 6: Image Upload (Optional)**

1. If required, upload an image of the product under the **Image** section.

2. Click on **Manage Images** to browse and select the image file from your computer.



---



### **Step 7: Save the Product**

1. After filling all the necessary details, scroll to the bottom of the page.

2. Click the **Save** button.

3. A confirmation message should appear stating that the product was successfully created.



---



### **Step 8: Sync Across POS Systems**

- The newly added item will automatically sync across all outlet POS systems within **10–30 seconds**.

- Verify the item’s availability at the POS or in the product list.



---



### **Additional Notes**

- Ensure all fields are filled accurately to avoid errors during synchronization.

- For bulk additions, consider using the import function if available.

- Always double-check Redeem SG Applicability settings before saving.
