# AI Support Metrics & Observability

Monitoring is critical to maintain system performance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Metrics

Resolution Rate Percentage of issues solved automatically

Escalation Rate Percentage requiring human intervention

Tool Success Rate How often tools return valid results

Hallucination Rate AI responses not grounded in data

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Target Benchmarks

Resolution Rate: 85--90% Escalation Rate: \<10% Hallucination Rate: \<2%

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Monitoring Dashboard

Recommended metrics dashboard:

-   daily ticket volume
-   AI handled tickets
-   human escalations
-   average resolution time

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Continuous Improvement

Log every interaction:

user_query retrieved_docs tools_used final_answer resolution_status
